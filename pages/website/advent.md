# Advent Calendar

![Advent calendar icon on the dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bmth-arg-wiki/wiki-assets/main/webpage/advent/advent-icon.png)

The advent calendar was released on the 15th of April, along with page 5 and 6 of [Skys' journal](skys-diary). 

The calendar has 13 shards, each connected to one of Bring Me The Horizon's Ascension 2 tour through North America. They were unlocked as shows progress. 
It also contains an older version of [M8](../m8) when opened far enough into the future, something that can be simulated by changing your system time.

![Advent menu on 15th of April 2026](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bmth-arg-wiki/wiki-assets/main/webpage/advent/advent-menu.png)
