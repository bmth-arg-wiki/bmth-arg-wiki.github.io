# Lo-files

Lo-files is an album containing lo-fi remixes of popular Bring Me The Horizon Songs. It features protagonist 
[Sof](../characters/sof) prominently on the cover, in a style that often appears with lo-fi studying streams.

![Lo-files album cover](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bmth-arg-wiki/wiki-assets/main/music/lofiles/lofiles.jpg)

***

## Lore relevance

Only minor lore connections have been discovered:

- canyoufeelmy<3.tmpx plays in the second area of the [M8 Museum](../for-sof/museum).
- The tracks are incorporated into a livestream connected to the [idle game](../website/idle), which it also 
shares aesthetic elements with.
