# M8's VR Museum

{% include content/info.html
title="Different museum versions"
content="Four versions of the museum have been released as of 13-04-2026. The cinema and stage 
were added in version 2 and updated in version 3. The aquarium was added in version 4. 
The contents of the main area have been shuffled around throughout the versions."
%}

M8's VR museum was originally released on the 30th of November 2025, hidden behind the lock-picking puzzle. 
This puzzle was previously hiding [Skys' diary](skys-diary), 
which was moved to its own section of the [website](../website/website) as a result. 

***

## Password Context

First, we need to get into the museum. Open the lock-picking puzzle from Sof's inventory.

1. Push the first four locks one by one until they spin with the first tool (medium-sized one)
2. Push the big lock until it spins with the second tool (big one)
3. Push the five smallest locks one by one until they spin on the right with the third tool (smallest one)

Note: you have to be gentle.

After completing the lock-picking puzzle, you will have access to the museum. 
Clicking the lift in the back left corner activates a password pop-up.

## Museum Contents

### Main Area - Museum Floor

The following items are labelled in the museum, from the back-left corner of the room:

- **Child's Drawings**: several drawings from Skys' diary.
- **Vintage Flyer**: a [Skys & the Starjammers poster](skystarjammers).
- **Newspaper**: [The Science Study newspaper](thesciencestudy).
- **Commemorative Statue (Date Unknown)**: a golden statue of the figure from the cover of Post Human: Survival Horror. 
The 3D model is the same as the [Survival Horror Tribute](survival-horror-anniversary). 
- **Post Human: Survival Horror (October 2020)**: the album cover of [Post Human: SURVIVAL HORROR](../music/ph-survival-horror).
- **Post Human: Nex Gen (May 2024)**: the album cover of [Post Human: NeX GEn](../music/ph-nex-gen).
- **Ritual Artifacts**: several artifacts depicted on the cover of PH:SH and its tribute 3D model.
- **Inia Geoffrensis (Amazon River Dolphin)**: an endangered species, also referred to as "pink dolphin", that 
can be found in the real world.
- **Metropolitan Cathedral of Our Lady Assumption and Saint Paul**: a 3D model of a real-life cathedral from Saõ Paulo.
- **Handroanthus Albus (Ipê)**: a tree with golden leaves, also inspired by a real-life species.

### Second Area - Forest
Although it looks like we used an elevator, we actually step outside into a wooded area. There are some smaller golden trees, 
many barren trees and a larger golden tree ahead. Past the golden tree we find a ghost - or hologram-like appearance of 
[Selene](../characters/selene), with the lo-fi track canyoufeelmy<3.tmpx playing as we get closer. 
Selene is made of (multiple layers of) ASCII art and the screen turns white when we get too close to her.

A sequence briefly appears on the screen: `qwertylkjsa`. It helped unlock the final tab of 
[Victor's interview](carmine_interview).

### Third Area - Cinema & Stage

The cinema and the stage were added in the January 20th 2026 update. This was also the day the São Paulo Live Immersive 
Virtual Experience was announced. M8 can now turn to face different angles, 
using Q and E on the keyboard to turn her 90 degrees.

The museum was rearranged to create some space for a mysterious door. The pose of the statue has changed and the 
Nex Gen album cover now has Sof looking sideways. Tickets have been placed between the Child's Drawings and the 
[Skys & the Starjammers](skystarjammers) poster. M8 will pick these up when she gets close enough. 
You can now go through the new mysterious portal door, which is a short hallway that leads to the cinema.

The cinema contains a screen with red patterns, with Oli performing in the middle. In the back row, several characters 
are visible: [Scott](../characters/scott), [Selene](../characters/selene), [M8](../m8), [Ren](../characters/ren), 
[Strassman](../characters/strassman), and [Dallus Lauren](../characters/dallus-lauren). If you sit in the front row 
to the left, you will now see DCPHR-2 flashing, with the code `153hg`. This was part of the cipher for the last tab of 
[Victor Carmine's interview](carmine_interview), although this cipher was already cracked by the time of release. 
If you go between the seats to pick up the M8 key card, it will make Oli disappear from the cinema screen.

With the M8 key card found, you will now be able to enter through the gates in front of the stage on the left and pass 
through the middle of the screen. It'll set you on a path which will allow you to travel to the stage, 
where we can see Oli perform.

### Third Area - Cinema & Stage, São Paulo L.I.V.E. Promotion Edition

The second update to the museum brought new content for the cinema area. The tickets to enter through the 
curtains have been placed right in front of them. The hallway now has a M8 popcorn bucket. 
You will see the bucket on the screen once you enter the cinema. It will ask you if you are ready for the movie, 
before loading a YouTube player with the first minutes of the show (mainly [DArkSide](../music/song-darkside)).

### Fourth Area - Aquarium

The aquarium was added on April 13th 2026, the same day that the re-recording of Count Your Blessings was announced. 
To access it, you just need to turn 180 degrees upon entering the museum, then head right through the blue portal door. 
You will find yourself in a pretty dilapidated aquarium, with a young girl staring at the fish in the main tank. 
If M8 turns her back to her then turns back around, the girl will have been replaced by a drawing. 

> __⚠︎ Speculation:__ the young girl could be Lyra ([Dallus Lauren's](../characters/dallus-lauren.md) daughter) 
> and the drawing looks like one of the Mares drawings from [Project D.U.S.T](project_dust.md).
